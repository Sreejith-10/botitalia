About Us

BOTITALIA stands as a prominent leader in Italy, specializing in the provision of top-tier commercial automotive parts, with a particular focus on high-quality truck and trailer brake pads for the independent aftermarket. Constantly evolving to meet market demands, our company boasts an extensive product range tailored for wholesale trade. Headquartered in Veneto, Italy, and strategically positioned regional offices in the UAE, along with distribution companies in France, the Middle East, Far East, and African continents, BOTITALIA is at the forefront of delivering reliable and on-demand services.

With a substantial stockholding of premium products and a market-leading catalogue, BOTITALIA takes pride in aligning with automotive aftermarket trends. Specializing in new model trucks and trailers, including notable brands like MERCEDES (Actros - mp1, mp2, mp3, mp4), VOLVO (FH-12, FH16, FM & FMX Series), MAN (F2000, TGA, TGX, TGS), SCANIA, IVECO, DAF, as well as trailers and Chinese trucks, we cater to a diverse range of commercial vehicles. Upholding a commitment to delivering OEM quality products, BOTITALIA ensures high-level service characterized by accessibility and excellence. Our ultimate goal is to be the singular solution for all your needs within the commercial vehicle aftermarket industry.
